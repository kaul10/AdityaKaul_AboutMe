My Story

I graduated with an Electronics Engineering degree at University of Mumbai. On completion of my undergraduate studies I began my journey in the professional world wherein I realized the importance of deriving insights from data in order to enhance business. Most of my work revolved around querying databases, business analysis and performance management. With the exponential rise in data as well as the rise of big data I felt that my knowledge on leveraging structured data was not sufficient. I wanted to analyze extremely large structured, semi structured and unstructured datasets to reveal patterns, trends and find correlation between variables.

With my final goal in mind I decided to pursue graduate studies at the Information School, University of Washington with a focus on Data Science and Analytics as well as Business Intelligence. Through this program I got an opportunity to not just strengthen my existing skills but also develop new skills that are a requirement for any aspiring Data Scientist. A large domain in itself, Data Science has several areas which I could have focused on but Data Visualization and Statistical Machine Learning caught my attention.

I spend a lot of time reading up on statistical methods and practicing the same in R Studio to ensure that I have practical knowledge of what I am reading. Also, I create interactive visualizations using D3 on sports data that I scrape off the internet which provides beautiful visuals to the otherwise number filled spreadsheets.
